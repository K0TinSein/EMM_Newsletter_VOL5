The little boy Mg Min Wai Oo, sixteen years old, is extremely suffering from pancreas inflammation. Since he was one-year-old, the gland in the pancreas became flat and the pancreas is now irritated and inflamed in enormous size, resulting in the bloating and swelling of his belly. His parents are encountering financial difficulties to proceed with the surgery.

Choon Toon approached Mytel to aid the little kid in the third week of October. Hence, Mytel has donated the required cash for his surgical costs and her mother has shown the words of thanks in tears, “I’m thankful Mytel for offering helping hands to us, I was so depressed and stressed out that I couldn’t afford for the surgery of my kid as a parent. I sincerely wish Mytel prosperity and success to increase in the future”. We wholeheartedly wish for a complete recovery from pancreas Inflammation and pray for the brighter future of him ahead.
